05. Reaction Kinetics

Rate Law: $r = k \cdot p_{SO_2} \cdot (p_{O_2} / p_{SO_3})^{0.5}$

Our reactor design utilizes **Vanadium Pentoxide ($V_2O_5$)** catalyst across a 4-bed adiabatic configuration. Equilibrium conversion is reached by cooling gas between Bed 3 and Bed 4 through the DCDA cycle.
